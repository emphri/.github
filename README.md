## 🌟 About Emphri

**Emphri** is a human-centered technology company dedicated to empowering individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) to lead more independent, confident, and fulfilling lives.

Our flagship product — the **Emphri AI Self-Companion App** — is an intelligent, adaptive assistant designed to be a trusted daily companion for users and peace-of-mind tool for their caregivers.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

> *"Foster independence and well-being through compassionate, human-centered artificial intelligence."*

We believe everyone deserves the tools to navigate daily life with confidence. Emphri bridges the gap between technology and accessibility, building products that are truly inclusive by design.

## ✨ Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🔔 **Smart Reminders** | Personalized routines and reminders to support daily living |
| 💬 **Communication Support** | Guidance for social engagement and emotion management |
| 🧠 **CBT-Based Interventions** | Evidence-based Cognitive Behavioral Therapy tools built into the experience |
| 🧘 **Mindfulness Exercises** | Calming techniques and wellness support at your fingertips |
| 📞 **Proactive Check-Ins** | Regular, caring check-ins via chat, voice notes, and calls |
| 🚨 **Emergency Tools** | Instant access to help when it's needed most |
| 👥 **Caregiver Dashboard** | Tools for caregivers to track progress and tailor user support |
